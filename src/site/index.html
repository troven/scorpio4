Semantic Integration & Automation


At the heart of Scorpio4 is a dynamic execution environment backed by an RDF model.

Everything in Scorpio4 is meta-data. We think about your data simply as meta-data about reality.

Meta-data helps us to understand what everything means and how to work with it.

Scorpio4 encourages and empowers you to:

  1. use URIs to identify everything of importance
  2. use URIs as tags to express relationships.
  3. Ask URIs what they mean using Semantic Web standards.
  4. use RDF to formally describe your problem domain
  5. use RDFS to give your data a richer "semantic" meaning
  6. use SPARQL to query, update or infer facts
  7. use templates to generate HTML, CSV, XML, SQL, SPARQL or source code.
  8. use Active Vocabularies to describe what to do with it all.
  9. (re)use Java, Ruby, Javascript, Groovy, Python, R libraries..
  10. Share URLs and therefore code, data and associate meaning with others.
