Passive Vocabularies

We use the word passive simply to contrast the Active Vocabularies, they can be incredibly powerful. In many use cases they are more useful than an Active Vocabulary.

That said, a Passive Vocabulary is simply a plain-ole RDFS vocabulary. These are semantic glue binding disparate data together.

There are hundreds of RDFS vocabularies that describe hundreds of problem domains, covering most industries, with various levels of maturity.

Scorpio4 supports any and all RDF-based vocabularies, with RDFS inferencing supported by default.

Scorpio4 can curate RDFS vocabularies from a variety of sources including SQL databases and Spreadsheets too.

The re-use of existing ontologies is encouraged by the semantic web community, and Scorpio4 too.
