Scorpio4

Actionable Knowledge

With Scorpio4, you create software by describing what you mean rather than how to do something.

Scorpio4 facilitates semantic integration and automates the communication between the application and it's environment.

Scorpio4 provides a rich suit of tools to extract, integrate, export, combine, transform, translate, visualise, execute and explore actionable knowledge.

At it's heart, Scorpio4 is a semantic middleware platform, leveraging the expressive power of semantic web technologies (RDF, RDFS, OWL and SPARQL)

RDF statements encode everything from data to behaviour, and express meaningful meta-data and relationships between any information, stored almost anywhere.

The underlying RDF data model is expressive, self-describing, schema-free and web-scale - based on the technologies at the heart of the Semantic Web.

Scorpio4 allows you to create domain-specific languages (Active Vocabularies) that describe what you mean to Scorpio4, your collaborators and other machines.

RDF allows Scorpio4 to capture complex domain models with a concise syntax and robust semantics.

You can make parts of your domain model executable using your choice of scripting language, Spring Beans or Enterprise Integration Patterns.

The Scorpio4 execution engine supports traditional programming techniques as well as it's own powerful declarative, event-driven programming paradigms.

Our goal is to empower domain experts to collaborate more closely on the design, develop, test, integrate, document and maintain highly integrated knowledge-driven work flows.
